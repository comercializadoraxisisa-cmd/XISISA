Services

We support your day-to-day operation so that supply stops being a risk and becomes a stable backbone: we coordinate purchasing, suppliers and light logistics so your team can focus on producing, selling and running the business.

Recurring supply

Ongoing management of daily or frequently used materials: we consolidate requests, follow up with suppliers and coordinate deliveries to your defined sites.

  • Consolidation of requests by area or project.
  • Coordination with multiple suppliers.
  • Basic visibility of what is consumed the most.

Special requirements

Sourcing and managing materials or spare parts that are not always easy to find in the market, whether due to technical specification, brand or presentation.

  • Equivalent alternatives when appropriate.
  • Support with technical data sheets and compatibility.
  • Clear communication of lead times and constraints.

Projects & planned shutdowns

Specific planning and supply for projects, major maintenance or shutdowns where timing is critical and work windows are limited.

  • Pre-planning of material lists.
  • Stage-by-stage supply aligned with your schedule.
  • Direct communication during execution.

Who do we work with?

Industrial environments

Manufacturing, logistics and transformation companies that require constant supplies and cannot stop their operation due to lack of material.

  • Industrial parks
  • Distribution centers
  • Production plants

Commercial & service environments

Specialized retailers, installation projects, maintenance contractors and service companies that need reliable supplies and fast response.

  • Maintenance
  • Contractors
  • Specialized retail

Companies looking for U.S.-sourced products

We support companies that need supplies or spare parts originally sourced from the United States and want a reliable way to obtain them without managing the import process themselves. We do not import directly — instead, we connect your operation with certified import suppliers and handle national logistics, coordinating clear and timely deliveries to your sites.

  • Specialized spare parts
  • Products not available in Mexico
  • Connection with import suppliers
  • National logistics

Service scope

Every company is different. Our model is designed to adapt to your operation: we can start with a limited group of materials or areas and grow as you see value in the service, always with direct and clear communication.
