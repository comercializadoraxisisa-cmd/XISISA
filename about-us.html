About us

A brief look at who we are and how we work with our customers.

Who we are

At COMERCIALIZADORA XISISA we work to make supply one less point of tension in your operation. We understand that when a production line stops due to lack of materials, it is not only time that is lost: money, trust and continuity with your customers are also at stake.

Our role is to stand between your operation and your suppliers, helping translate daily needs into clear, organized requests with proper follow-up. We are a trading company with an operational focus, not just buy–sell.

We operate with a compact, approachable team that prioritizes clear communication and quick response. We prefer to tell you in advance when something gets complicated, instead of letting you find out at the last minute.

In a nutshell

  • A trading company focused on keeping your business supplied without stopping.
  • We coordinate suppliers, timing and deliveries for your day-to-day operation.
  • Active presence in Monterrey Metro Area, Reynosa and Guadalajara Metro Area.
  • Supply
  • Light logistics
  • Daily operation

How we operate

We keep your daily supply more organized and predictable, without overcomplicating your internal processes.

1

We understand your operation

We start with a simple assessment of your operation: materials, frequency and which items are critical.

  • Identification of critical and support supplies.
  • Delivery windows, schedules and site constraints.
  • Specific requirements by area, project or site.
2

We define catalog and criteria

Together we agree which products to handle, which brands to prioritize and how to deal with substitutions.

  • Base catalog by product family.
  • Target brands and approved equivalents.
  • Clear price ranges and commercial conditions.
3

We run your supply

We handle the day-to-day work: requests, purchasing, supplier coordination and deliveries.

  • We group requests by area or project.
  • We coordinate suppliers and carriers.
  • We monitor deliveries and report deviations.
4

We measure and adjust

We periodically review key indicators and fine-tune the way we work with you.

  • Regular review of basic service indicators.
  • Adjustments to catalog and service levels.
  • Visibility of the items that move the most in your operation.

Our service principles

What guides us in every decision we make regarding your orders, suppliers and delivery times.

Operational reliability

We know your operation cannot stop. We prioritize continuity over one-off sales, looking for alternatives when things get complicated and letting you know in advance if there is a real risk.

Visibility and communication

You should know the status of your key requests without having to chase anyone. A timely call or message is always better than a surprise on delivery day.

Responsible flexibility

We only propose substitutions when they truly add value: same performance, same function or better, always respecting your quality standards and internal policies.

Where we operate

Our operation covers three strategic regions that allow us to respond quickly, flexibly and reliably to our customers’ needs. These areas include industrial, logistics and commercial hubs where operational continuity is critical.

Monterrey Metropolitan Area Reynosa Guadalajara Metropolitan Area

If your operation is located outside these regions, we can assess the feasibility of serving you, depending on the type of materials, delivery frequency and logistics conditions involved. Our goal is to adapt to your operation, not the other way around.
